Guide
How to use My Sambandh
1
Register for free
Click "Join free" and fill in your basic details — who the profile is for (self, son, daughter, etc.), religion, language, date of birth, and location. Registration takes about 2 minutes.
2
Verify your mobile number
You'll receive a 6-digit OTP via SMS. Enter it to activate your account. If you don't receive it within 10 minutes, call +91 94277 90877.
3
Complete your profile
Add photos, educational background, career, family details, and partner preferences. A complete profile gets 3x more responses.
4
Browse & filter matches
Use our search filters — religion, community, location, age, education, and more — to find compatible profiles. Automated matches are shown daily.
5
Upgrade to a paid plan (optional)
Free members can browse. Paid plans unlock private messaging, expert-picked matches, conversation management, and the Pehli Mulaqat grooming session.
6
Connect safely
Chat through our platform — your mobile number is never shared unless you choose to. Elite members have their relationship manager initiate contact on their behalf.
7
Meet & engage
We organize your first meeting and prepare you with grooming advice. Our support continues right up to engagement.
